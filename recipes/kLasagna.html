Keto Lasagna

"A disgustingly gooey and delicious keto-friendly lasagna"

The keto-friendly lasagna recipe that will change your life AND your waste-line! This recipe will satisfy any cheese-lover's cravings and dietary goals! (Warning: Not lactose-intolerant-friendly)

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup lean ground beef (cooked)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pink salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 zucchini (large)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Add coconut oil to a medium high heat pan and cook the ground beef. Add your favorite sauces to the ground beef. After 2 minutes, remove and set aside.
  3. Slice your zucchini into many tiny bite-sized pieces, ideally using a mandolin slicer.
  4. Add the ricotta, Parmesan, garlic cloves, salt, pepper, and egg to a blender or food processor. Blend until indistinguishable. This is the good stuff.
  5. Spray a light coating of oil on an oven safe skillet or casserole dish, and then add a layer of ground beef.
  6. Now place two layers of zucchini criss-cross. This will help evenly cook the food and for the zucchini noodles to cover the full dish.
  7. Be unshy and add a buttload of ricotta mixture on top of dat.
  8. Sprinkle on some shredded mozzarella.
  9. Repeat this layering a second time, and ensure that the last layer is mozzarella cheese.
  10. Now bake the thing in the oven for 30 minutes or until the cheese is browned. You like it more brown? My brother/sister. Just leave it on a low broil for 2-3 minutes (But no more than 5). Sit and watch it closely.
