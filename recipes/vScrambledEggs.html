Vegan Scrambled Eggs

"I Can't Believe It's Not Scrambled Eggs!"

"Scrambled eggs" made from chickpea flour, soy milk, nutritional yeast, turmeric, dijon mustard, garlic powder, black salt, and onion powder.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Medium Onion White, Yellow, or Brown, Chopped
  • 1 Medium Tomato, Chopped
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Mushrooms, Sliced
  • 1/2 cup Chickpea Flour
  • 1/2 cup Soy Milk (120ml) or other non-dairy milk
  • 2 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Black Salt (Kala Namak)
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
