Vegan Scrambled Eggs

"I Can't Believe It's Not Scrambled Eggs!"

"Scrambled eggs" made from chickpea flour, soy milk, nutritional yeast, turmeric, dijon mustard, garlic powder, black salt, and onion powder.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Medium Onion White, Yellow, or Brown, Chopped
  • 1 Medium Tomato, Chopped
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Mushrooms, Sliced
  • 1/2 cup Chickpea Flour
  • 1/2 cup Soy Milk (120ml) or other non-dairy milk
  • 2 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Black Salt (Kala Namak)
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder

Steps:

  1. Place a non-stick frying pan on heat. Add olive oil and chopped onion and sauté until the onions are soft.
  2. Add in the chopped tomato and sliced mushrooms and sauté until softened.
  3. Mix your mix by adding chickpea flour, soy milk, nutritional yeast, turmeric, dijon mustard, garlic powder, black salt, and onion powder to a measuring jug and whip it all good.
  4. Pour the mix over the vegetables and cook until the mixture begins to firm up, akin to a pancake. Then begin breaking it bad and scramble it with a spatula until it all looks well-done and not wet and runny.
  5. If it sticks, throw some more oil on it.
  6. Serve with toasted ciabatta, fresh avocado and sprinkes of black pepper and chives, or just add whatever the hell you want to it.
